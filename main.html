Welcome to the Rural Healthcare Assistant Web App

Our web app is designed to bridge the gap in healthcare for rural communities by providing a simple, efficient platform for rural doctors to manage patient records, appointments, and communication with patientsâ€”all while operating on low bandwidth and across platforms.

This solution allows a single doctor to:
  • Keep track of patient histories and treatment progress.
  • Receive regular alerts for patient visits and appointments.
  • Reschedule appointments with ease when necessary.
  • Access the app from multiple devices with secure credentials.

For patients, the app makes healthcare more accessible:
  • Book appointments with a single click.
  • Receive appointment reminders and alerts.
  • Send emergency messages to the doctor.
  • View their appointment schedules and health records.

With our solution, doctors can monitor changes in patient health and provide timely treatment based on real-time data. This ensures that healthcare in rural areas becomes more efficient, responsive, and patient-centered.

Our goal is to empower rural doctors to deliver better healthcare, while ensuring patients have a direct, easy way to access care when they need it most.
